How to care for and maintain non slip shoes?

Everyday slip resistant shoes are an essential part of any workwear wardrobe. They offer an important level of protection against accidental slips and falls.

But, taking proper care of them is also essential in order to maintain their integrity and effectiveness.

Here are a few simple tips on how to care for and maintain your non slip shoes:

  • Clean and wipe down the shoes regularly. Rinse off any dirt and grime, then use a damp cloth and mild soap to clean the surface. Wipe away any excess water.
  • After cleaning, thoroughly dry your non slip shoes, as moisture can harm the non slip surface and render them less effective.
  • Inspect the sole of your shoes regularly. When the sole begins to wear down, replace it promptly in order to preserve your shoes and the non slip surface.
  • If the shoe uppers become stained or wet, remove them and let them dry thoroughly before wearing them again.
  • Store your non slip shoes in a cool, ventilated area to reduce the damaging effects of humidity.
  • Following these simple steps will help to extend the life of your non slip shoes, whilst helping to keep you safe.
