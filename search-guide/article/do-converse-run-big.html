How To Care for and Maintain Converse?

Taking care of your Converse can make them last much longer and remain comfortable and stylish, so it's important to know the right way to clean and maintain yours.

To keep your Converse looking, feeling and performing like new, here are some tips for keeping them in good condition:

  • Clean your Converse regularly. Use a mild soap and warm water and lightly scrub the exterior of the shoes. Gently remove any dirt or marks with a soft cloth.
  • Let your Converse air dry. Once you're done cleaning them, let them air dry at room temperature, away from direct heat and sunlight. Stuffing the shoes with newspaper can help absorb some of the moisture.
  • Protect them from the elements. Use a waterproof spray on your Converse before heading out in the rain or snow to prevent water damage.
  • Polish your shoes. Use a neutral or suede shoe polish on canvas shoes or a leather polish or conditioner on leather shoes.
  • Don't forget the interior. Over time, the interior of your Converse can become filled with dirt, sweat and other debris. Use an old toothbrush and a mild soap to scrub away grime and odor and then air dry.
  • Replace the insoles regularly. Check your insoles periodically and replace them if necessary. This will help keep your feet comfortable and reduce your risk of foot injury.

Following these simple tips can help you keep your Converse in good condition and improve their longevity.

Taking care of your shoes will help you enjoy their comfort and style for years to come.
