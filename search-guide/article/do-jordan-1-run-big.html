Measuring for Jordan 1:

When looking to buy a pair of the iconic Jordan 1 shoes, it is important to ensure you measure your feet correctly to find a pair close to your size.

By taking accurate measurements and following these simple instructions you can determine exactly what size of Jordan 1 shoes you need:

  • Put on a pair of socks that you plan to wear with the shoes.
  • Place a ruler flat against the wall and stand with your heel flush against the wall.
  • Place a blank piece of paper on the floor under your foot and make a pencil mark on the paper at the end of your longest toe.
  • Measure from the edge of the paper to the pencil mark and use this to determine the length of your feet in centimeters.
  • Remember to apply this same measurement technique for each foot, as it is common for one foot to be slightly larger than the other.
  • Once you have found the length of both feet in centimeters, divide this measurement by two and round up to determine your shoe size.
  • Consult a Jordan 1 size chart to check the exact size you need.

How To Care for and Maintain Jordan 1?

Maintaining your Jordan 1 sneakers is essential for preserving their quality and style.

To keep these iconic shoes clean and looking like new, there are a few simple steps to follow.

Here are some tips for caring for and maintaining your Jordan 1 sneakers, so you can enjoy their comfort and style for many years to come:

  • Cleaning: The first step in caring for your Jordan 1 is to give them a regular cleaning. Use a soft brush and damp cloth to remove any dirt and debris from the exterior. Take special care to clean the tongue and laces, as they can get particularly soiled. If necessary, you can use a gentle, mild soap on the cloth to help break down tough dirt, but be sure to avoid harsh chemicals as they can damage the material.
  • Deodorizing: To keep your Jordan 1 smelling fresh, sprinkle a light dusting of baking soda inside the shoe and leave it overnight. Then, brush off the baking soda in the morning and your shoes will be lightly deodorized.
  • Protecting: To protect your Jordan 1 from the elements and stains, you should apply a water-repellant protectant spray to the exterior. This will help keep water and dirt from damaging or staining the material. Be sure to let the protectant dry completely before wearing.
  • Drying: After cleaning your Jordan 1, it’s important to make sure the interior is completely dry before wearing. If the shoe is damp, stuff it with newspaper or a shoe tree to draw out the moisture and prevent odor buildup.

Following these simple tips and tricks will help ensure that your Jordan 1 sneaker remain looking their best for years to come.

With proper care, you’ll be able to experience the style and comfort of this iconic sneaker for a long time.
