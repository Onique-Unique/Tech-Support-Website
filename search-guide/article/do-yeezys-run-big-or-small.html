Measuring for Yeezy's:

Finding the right size in Yeezy's shoes can sometimes be rough. Fortunately, proper measurement and following the right guidelines can make the process easier and help you get the right fit and optimal comfort.

Here are some tips for measuring your feet for Yeezy's:

  • Make sure you have the proper footwear for measuring. Wearing socks will add extra thickness to your feet, and impair the accuracy of the measurements.
  • Trace the outline of both of your feet on a piece of paper with a pencil. Make sure your feet are flat, with no gaps in between your heel and toes.
  • Measure the height of your arches from the highest point to the deepest point in the centre.
  • Measure the length and width of both feet from the farthest points.
  • Record all measurements, as well as the widths of your ankles, calves and thighs.
  • Compare the measurements with Yeezy's sizing charts to find the closest fit.

By using these tips, you can find the best Yeezy size for your feet.

Remember that the measurements may vary depending on your style and preferences.

Also, consider buying some shoe insoles if your feet require supplemental support.

How To Care for and Maintain Yeezy's?

Whether you’ve just got your first pair of Yeezy’s, or want to make sure you get the most out of an existing pair, it pays to put in the effort to keep them in the best condition possible.

After all, Yeezy footwear is made with premium materials and can last for a long time if cared for properly.

To keep your Yeezy’s looking fresh, here are some tips:

  • Wear protective shoe sprays to resist soiling and water damage.
  • Use a soft toothbrush and non-abrasive detergent to spot clean areas where dirt is visible.
  • Re-apply protective sprays regularly.
  • Use a soft cloth to buff away any dust or dirt particles that have embedded itself in the material.
  • Stuff clean newspapers inside the shoes when storing them to help them retain shape and reduce wear-and-tear from transport or everyday use.
  • Avoid exposing your Yeezy's to intense heat, humidity or direct sunlight.
  • Allow them to air out between wears and store them in a cool, dry place when not in use.

By following these tips and taking special care of your Yeezy's, you can keep them in great condition for many wears to come.
