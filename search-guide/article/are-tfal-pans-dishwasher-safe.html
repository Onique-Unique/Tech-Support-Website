How to properly load TFAL Pans in a dishwasher?

Loading TFAL Pans in a dishwasher properly can help to ensure that they come out thoroughly clean.

Here are some helpful tips on how to make it happen:

  • Thoroughly clean the pans inside and out before loading them in the dishwasher. This helps to prevent greasy residue from contaminating other dishes.
  • Place TFAL Pans in the lower rack of the dishwasher with the coated sides of the pans facing down. This prevents the pans from being scratched by the spray arms.
  • Ensure that the pans are spaced evenly and not overlapping with each other. This allows water to circulate and clean dishes thoroughly.
  • For best results, use the gentle cycle with a mild detergent. This ensures that the pans won't be scratched or damaged during the dishwashing cycle.
  • After the dishwashing cycle is complete, promptly remove the pans from the dishwasher and dry them thoroughly by hand. Leaving wet pans in the dishwasher can lead to rust and corrosion.
