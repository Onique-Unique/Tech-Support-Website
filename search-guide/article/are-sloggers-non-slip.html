How to care for and maintain Sloggers?

Maintaining and caring for your Sloggers is essential for keeping them in good condition and making them last as long as possible.

Here are some tips for ensuring your non slip shoes stay in top shape:

  • Clean and Dry Them Properly: After each wear, wipe down your Sloggers with a damp cloth and allow them to air dry fully. If your shoes are muddy or heavily soiled, gently rinse and scrub them with warm water and a mild soap before air drying.
  • Replace Worn Out Soles: Letting your soles wear down can cause serious injury, so as soon as you start to feel lumps or other signs of wear when walking in your Sloggers, it’s time to replace the soles.
  • Clean the Inside of Your Shoes Regularly: Use a small brush and mild soap to regularly scrub down the inside of your shoes and remove any buildup of dirt or debris.
  • Keep Your Shoes Away From Heat: Avoid leaving your Sloggers in direct sunlight or in other areas where they are exposed to extreme temperatures, as this can cause the rubber to deteriorate.
  • Treat Your Shoes With Waterproofing Spray: Give your Sloggers a protective boost by applying a waterproofing spray.

By following these simple steps, your Sloggers are sure to last you for many years to come.
