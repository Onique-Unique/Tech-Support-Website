What are the Safety issues with cleaning Vitamix in a dishwasher?

Cleaning a Vitamix blender in a dishwasher can be a great way to ensure a deep clean without having to do it manually.

However, there are a few safety concerns when it comes to cleaning Vitamix containers in the dishwasher.

Here are five safety issues to be aware of before cleaning your Vitamix in the dishwasher:

  • An overheated dishwasher could damage the container, leaving it warped and unsafe to use.
  • A dishwasher could damage the blade, causing the container to work inefficiently, or even causing the blade to break if it overheats.
  • The hot water used may crack the Vitamix container.
  • Vitamix containers require a specific lubricant to be used when cleaning, and the dishwasher may not be strong enough to remove it, leading to a risky build-up.
  • Hot water, especially when run for a long time, could potentially lead to permanent discoloration of the container.
