What materials are dishwasher safe and why to avoid others?

Dishwasher safe materials are essential for keeping your dishware and kitchen appliances sanitary and looking their best.

  • Silverware: Silverware is often more vulnerable to corrosion in a dishwasher, so it's best to wash it by hand.

What are the Safety issues with cleaning Brita in a dishwasher?

Cleaning Brita filters in a dishwasher may seem like a great, quick way to ensure you have a clean and healthy water filter, but there are a few safety issues to consider.

Here are the potential risks of using a dishwasher to clean a Brita filter:

  • Damage to the Brita Filter: Heat, water pressure, and scrubbing could damage the filter elements over time, reduce it's efficiency, or decrease it's lifespan.
  • Damage to the Dishwasher: The pressure and grit from the filter could cause damage to the dishwasher mechanism, leading to costly repairs.
  • Bacterial Contamination:The filter could become contaminated with bacteria from the dishwashing process, leading to the possibility of spreading germs from the dishwasher to the filter.
  • Possible Injury: Due to the high pressurized water involved, there is a risk of injury when cleaning a Brita filter in a dishwasher.
