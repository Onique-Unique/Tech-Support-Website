What are the Safety issues with Adidas Superstars?

Adidas Superstars remain an iconic part of the streetwear and sneaker culture, but they can also present some safety issues that are worth considering before making your purchase.

Here are some safety issues to keep in mind when wearing Adidas Superstars:

  • Traction: The rubber outsoles of Adidas Superstars are known to reduce traction when ankle sprains and slips are most likely to happen.
  • Sole durability: Superstars remain durable shoes overall, but consistent wear and tear can cause them to wear down quicker, making them less supportive.
  • Nonbreathable fabric: Superstars are made of leather and canvas materials, which can cause the foot to become sweaty and uncomfortable.
  • Lack of arch support: Superstars feature limited arch support, which can result in pain in the feet, legs, and back.
  • Wrong size: It is important to get your exact size when it comes to Superstars and any other sneakers for that matter, otherwise it can cause blisters to form on the feet.

It is essential to consider these safety issues before purchasing a pair of Adidas Superstars and wear them with caution.
