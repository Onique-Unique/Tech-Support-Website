How do you apply ceramic interior coating?

The application of ceramic inside coating is not as complicated as it appears. In actuality, it is a simple procedure that can be accomplished in a few stages. The first step is to ensure that the surface is clear of dirt, dust, and debris. Before applying the ceramic coating, the surface should be completely dry.

The ceramic coating is then mixed according to the label's specifications. Make sure to fully combine everything so that there are no clumps. After it has been combined, apply it to the surface with a brush or a roller. Apply it in a thin coat and avoid applying it too thickly.

Allow the first layer to completely dry before applying a second coat. After the second layer has dried, apply a sealer to protect the coating.
