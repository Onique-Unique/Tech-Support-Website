How does Ceramic Coating work?

Ceramic Coating is a nanotechnology-based protective coating that is applied to automotive paintwork to protect it from environmental damage, including UV radiation, bird droppings, acid rain and road salt.

The coating forms a microscopically thin, transparent layer that shields the paint from these damaging elements. It also enhances the appearance of the paintwork, making it shinier and smoother. Ceramic Coating is available as a stand-alone product or as part of the Tesla Paint Protection Film.

How do I apply Ceramic Coating on Tesla?
