What do airlines do with extra luggage fees?

>>> It has been a long-standing debate over what airlines do with the extra luggage fees, and for expanation many people argue that it is because the airline needs to recover the cost of baggage handling and storage.

Airlines have to pay for employees to handle and transport baggage, as well as rent or buy space to store it. This cost can add up, so it only makes sense that airlines would pass some of that cost on to passengers.

Others argue that the real reason airlines charge extra baggage fee is to make more money. They say that the cost of handling and storing baggage is relatively small, and that the true motive for the fee is to get passengers to pay more for their tickets. This way, the airline can make more money without raising ticket prices.

Which view is correct? There is no clear answer, although it is likely that both factors are in play. Airlines want to grow their earnings even though handling and storing baggage costs money. By charging a fee for checked bags, both goals are achieved.

What Airline has the best baggage policy?

When choosing an airline, one of the most important things to consider is their baggage policy. There are different policies for each airline, some of which are more inconvenient than others. Consequently, which airline has the best bag policy?

When evaluating an airline's luggage policy, there are a few variables to take into account. The first is the cost of checked luggage with the airline. 

There are now several airlines that charge for checked bags, and the costs can be anything between $10 and $50 per bag. The number of luggage you are permitted to check is the second factor to take into account.

One checked bag is the maximum allowed by some carriers, while two or more are permitted. The size of the bags is the third factor to take into account. While some airlines have size restrictions, others have weight restrictions.

  • JetBlue is one of the least expensive airlines when it comes to luggage costs. For the first bag, they charge $20, and for the second, they charge $35. Additionally, they offer free bag check for a total of two bags. 
  • Another inexpensive carrier is United Airlines, which charges $25 for the first bag and $35 for the second. Additionally, they offer free bag check for a total of two bags.
  • When it comes to size restrictions, Southwest Airlines is one of the best option. They do not have any size restrictions on their checked bags. However, they do have a weight restriction of 50 pounds per bag.

Tips for packing light.

There are a few important considerations to make when it comes to packing lightly. Consider the kinds of activities you'll be engaging in before your vacation. 

  • You should take heavier items like a jacket and sturdy shoes if you plan to do a lot of trekking or visiting cold regions. But you can get away with bringing a smaller luggage if you visit warmer regions as warmer clothing tends to be much thinner than cold weather clothing.
  • Think how often you'll be able to wash laundry while on your trip if it is an extended stay away. You'll need to bring more clothes than if you're simply travelling for three days if you won't have access to a washing machine during that time.
  • Finally, try to pack versatile items that can be worn multiple ways. For example, a dress can be worn as a bottom or a top depending on the versatility. A scarf can be worn as a shawl or a headband. And a cardigan can be worn as a sweater or a jacket.

By following these tips, you can pack light and still have everything you need for a great trip.

What is - The best luggage for air travel?

Rolling suitcases, duffel bags, backpacks, and soft-sided baggage are some of the most popular types of luggage for air travel.
