Binance Maker or Taker Explained:


Are you a - "Maker" or "Taker"?

Makers vs takers
So Makers make & Takers take right?

Not Necessarily

 Heres Why ↓

  • When you look at the Binance fee layout, you can notice that there are various fees that depends on if you're a "maker" or a "taker" and this can significantly sway you towards the ultimate decision you make.
  • Maker trades occur when an order is placed that is partially or completely placed on the order book, such as a limit order placed via the trading screen on binance.com. 
  • Any trades that follow from that order will be maker trades. The name comes from the fact that these orders add some volume to the order book, assisting in the "making" of the market.
  • Now - Taker trades happens when an order trades immediately before being placed on the order book, whether the order is partially or fully filled, regardless these transactions "take" volume off the order book.
