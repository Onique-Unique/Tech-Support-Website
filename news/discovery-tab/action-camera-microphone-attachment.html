Action Camera Microphone Attachments!

When it comes to action cameras, finding a good microphone attachment can be a challenge. The quality of the audio recorded will depend on the type of attachment you choose, as well as the type of camera you have.

Some of the best options will have noise-cancelling features and offer high-quality audio that is clear and detailed. It's important to do your research and read reviews to find the right attachment for your needs.

Using an action camera mic is a great way to get improved sound quality for your projects.

With improved sound clarity, directionality, and options for customizing your audio quality, there are plenty of benefits to take advantage of.

Here are just a few of the ways you can benefit from using an action camera microphone attachment:

  • Get crystal-clear audio: An action camera microphone attachment lets you capture high-quality audio along with your video clips. No more worrying about muffled or distorted audio – you can get the crisp, clear sound you want.
  • Create immersive films: With an action camera microphone attachment, you can make action films that put the viewer right in the middle of the action. Every sound will be amplified and more significant, so the immersive feel of a film is deeper and more impactful.
  • Record sound from unexpected places: An action camera microphone attachment is perfect for recording audio from locations where you wouldn’t normally get great sound. For example, you can use it to get sound from underwater or from extreme heights or depths.
  • Easy setup: With an action camera microphone attachment, setting up and recording your audio is incredibly easy. Just attach the microphone and you’re ready to start recording the perfect sound for your video.
  • Enhanced Sound Quality: Action camera mics can help to enhance the sound quality of your videos and photos. Not only will improved sound clarity make your clips appear more professional, but also it will make your projects more enjoyable to listen to.
  • Improved Sound Directionality: Action camera mics allow you to focus sound more accurately. This means if you’re wanting the viewer to hear a particular sound, such as an actor's dialogue, the action camera’s mic can allow you to capture that, while controlling unwanted noise.
  • Expanded Audio Options: Action camera mics expand your audio options. With a variety of sizes and designs to choose from, you can find a mic attachment that’s tailored to your needs and preferences. You can choose from a variety of polarity patterns and even mount them for 360-degree sounds.

Tips for getting the best audio quality from your action camera microphone attachment:

  • Invest in a high quality microphone attachment for your action camera. Look for ones that have shock absorption and wind noise reduction for the clearest audio.
  • Adjust the placement of the microphone so that it is pointed directly towards the source of sound.
  • Wind noise can be a major factor in reducing audio quality. If possible, record indoors or use a wind muff to protect the microphone from wind noise.
  • Make sure to test your audio levels before starting to record. Make sure they are not too high or too low.
  • To reduce background noise, use a directional microphone, or place the microphone in an area with minimal background noise.
  • Experiment with different microphone levels to see which works best for the type of video you're recording.
  • Integrate a post-production audio editor into your workflow if you are unable to achieve the desired sound quality in the field. This will allow you to adjust frequencies, noise reduction, and other parameters to get the best sound possible.
  • Lastly, make sure to use the proper audio cables. Be sure to use gold-plated connectors and the right connector type (i.e. 3.5 mm or 6.5 mm). This will help ensure better sound quality.
