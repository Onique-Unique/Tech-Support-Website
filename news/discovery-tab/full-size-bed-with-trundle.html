Guide: Full Size Bed With Trundle!

A full size bed with trundle is a great option for those looking for comfort and versatility in their bedroom.

The trundle provides an extra sleeping space, making it ideal for guests or as a multi-functional storage area. The full size bed provides ample room for comfortable sleeping, making it a great choice for those looking to upgrade their bedding.

Trundle beds are a great way to maximize space in your home and give you extra sleeping space when you have guests.

There are several different types of trundle beds available, so you can find the perfect style for your home.

  • One type of trundle bed is a daybed with trundle. A daybed works as a full-size bed by day, and you can use the trundle underneath to store bedding or toys. On nights when you need the extra sleeping space, you can simply pull out the trundle to create an extra sleeping surface.
  • Another type of trundle bed is a platform bed frame with a pull out trundle underneath. This type of trundle bed is perfect for smaller bedrooms as it will give you a full-size bed when you need it and a twin bed when you don’t. You can also store bedding, toys, or clothes in the trundle in between uses.
  • Finally, you can also get bunk beds with built-in trundles. These beds are great for both adults and kids, as you can have a twin-size bed on top, a full-size bed on the bottom, and a trundle that can stay tucked out of sight below.

No matter which type of trundle bed you choose, you’ll have extra sleeping space and storage space for all your needs.




Advantages of having a trundle in your bed:

If you are looking to upgrade your bedroom without having to invest too much money, a trundle bed is the perfect solution.

A trundle bed is a great space-saving solution, providing extra sleeping space without taking up too much extra space.

Here are some of the key advantages of having a trundle in your bed:

  • Income opportunities - For Rental/ Airbnb Hosts: If you are short on space, but you need room for extra guests, a trundle bed can provide additional income opportunities. Having a trundle bed in your home means that you can offer your guests extra space when needed, as well as a comfortable and convenient place for them to stay.
  • Comfort - When you are lying in your trundle bed, you will be able to enjoy the added comfort of having a mattress that is higher than the main bed. This allows you to get a better night's sleep, as the raised bed helps to reduce back pressure and neck pain.
  • Practical - Not only is a trundle bed great for saving space, it also makes it much easier to move beds around the house when you need to rearrange furniture. The wheels allow you to move the bed easily, without requiring much effort.
  • Versatile - Trundle beds come in a variety of styles and sizes to suit any space, and can even double as a couch during the day. This makes them a great multi-purpose piece of furniture for any home.

Overall, trundle beds are a great choice for anyone looking to make the most out of their space.

They provide extra comfort and versatility, making them a great addition to any bedroom.



Tips for making the most of your full size bed with trundle:

  • Maximize storage space by buying full size storage beds with drawers. This is perfect for couples who share a bed, as both sides will have plenty of extra storage for blankets, clothes, and extra pillows.
  • Splurge on cozy bedding and a mattress topper to make the most out of your full size trundle bed. It’s important to invest in quality products so you can get a good nights sleep.
  • Place durable and decorative trunks at the foot of the bed. Use them to store out of season clothing, books, or extra toys.
  • Position low-profile furniture underneath the trundle bed. This will provide additional seating or storage space without making the room feel cramped.
  • Add a few statement pieces like a headboard or bench. Draw attention to the bed by making sure it’s the focus of your bedroom.
  • Utilize wall space rather than floor space. Put up shelves, cabinets, or wall art to make your room appear more spacious.
  • Layer your bedding with one large blanket, one medium blanket, a bedspread, and decorative pillows. This will create a cozy and inviting bed.
  • Experiment with different colors and patterns. This could be just the inspiration you need to transform your room into one you truly love.
