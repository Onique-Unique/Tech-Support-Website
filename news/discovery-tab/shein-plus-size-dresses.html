Guide: To Shein Plus Size Dresses

Finding the perfect fit in plus size dresses from Shein can be a challenge, but with proper measurements and careful consideration of the size chart, you can greatly increase your chances of finding the perfect dress for your body type.

Additionally, reading customer reviews and checking for stretchy or adjustable materials can also help ensure a comfortable and flattering fit.

Reading customer reviews of plus size clothing options is an essential step when considering making a purchase.

Reviews provide insight into the quality, fit, and comfort of a piece of clothing, so you can be sure you'll be satisfied with your purchase.

Reviews can also tell you how true to size the product is, how it's been sized and how it looks on someone with a similar body type to you.

By reading customer reviews, you can get a firsthand, unbiased opinion about the product, so that you have a better idea of what you're getting for your money.





Tips for measuring your body correctly for online clothing purchases:

  • Take Accurate Measurements: To ensure you receive the best fit for your body, carefully measure your chest, waist, hips, and inseam with a tailor’s tape measure and jot down the measurements. Doing so will greatly help when ordering items online.
  • Look at the Size Chart: Most online stores provide a size chart that can help you get the right fit. These size charts usually include body measurements like your chest, waist and hips measurements along with the corresponding sizes.
  • Consider Your Body Type: Everyone’s body is different and it’s important to factor in your body type when determining your size. The same size could fit differently depending on whether you’re curvy, petite, broad-shouldered etc.
  • Read Reviews: Reading customer reviews is a great way to get an idea of how a certain garment fits. Customers often provide helpful reviews about the fitment of certain items which allows you to order accordingly.
  • Choose the Right Material: Different materials have different fitment. Be sure to read the fabric descriptions so you’ll know what kind of fit to expect.
  • Order a Bigger Size: If you’re unsure of the size, it’s better to err on the side of caution and opt for a bigger size. You can always get an alteration if it’s too big or tailor it at home.
  • Ask For Help: Last but not least, don’t hesitate to reach out for help if you’re unsure of what size to order. Most online stores have style advisors who are more than capable of helping you out in finding the perfect fit.



Key features to look for in a plus size dress for a flattering fit:

  • A waist-cinching empire waistline: Waistlines that sit at the natural waist can create some gorgeous curves! Plus size dresses with an empire waistline are an ideal way to make the most of your shape.
  • A V-neckline: V-necks are excellent for showing off curves in all the right places, and they produce a slimming effect.
  • Flowing, A-line skirts: Skirts that fall away from the body give more room without squeezing, while still emphasizing a flattering figure.
  • Wrap-style designs: Wrap-style designs hug the body in all the right places without becoming too tight, and they also look beautiful when coupled with other styles.
  • Rhinestone and sequin accents: A little bit of sparkle can really liven up any look—just choose the right places to accessorize with rhinestones and sequins.
  • Back details: A deep V-back or another eye-catching detail can create visual intrigue for onlookers, and make that dress unforgettable.
