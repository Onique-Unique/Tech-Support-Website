Pre registration sign up - for Rainbow Six (6) Mobile

Community Post

 April 7, 2022

Calling all "Operators" to join force - Alpha Pre Test, Ubisoft Rainbow six team announced an opportunity for the public to be invited for sign up of the Alpha. Early signups may guarantee your spot to be a part of the first set to get their hands on "Rainbow Six Mobile", Players who are interested can sign up now through Pre registration.

Rainbow Six Mobile to boast mechanism similar to its predecessor and will look to bring excitement to the mobile genre. Ubisoft have already opened Pre-registration for Rainbow Six Mobile and will be looking forward to move into the next phase during spring.

What you will need to join Alpha Pre test - valid registered Ubisoft account. If selected, an informed email will be sent out to you. "All selected participants will receive step-by-step instructions on how to get their mobile device ready".

A Limited amount of play testers will be selected to assess - "key features, core gameplay mechanics, server stability and online infrastructure".The dev team will collect any feedback and suggestions from play testers for further developement and improvements of the Rainbow Six Mobile experience before the launch
