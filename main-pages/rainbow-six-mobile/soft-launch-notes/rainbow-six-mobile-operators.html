Rainbow Six (6) Mobile Upcoming Operators in Alpha Test

Community Post

 April 14, 2022 • 3 min read

Ubisoft has made it clear that Rainbow Six (6) Mobile is set to launch during this year - with it's "Alpha Test" expected to take the lead this current spring.

Rainbow Six (6) Siege has goals of emulating it's Console and PC version of the game to Mobile platform expanding it's reach further into the hands of on the go Gamers.

Keep reading to learn more...

Featured ad spot by google

Rainbow Six (6) Mobile will have characters that are unique to the game known as Operators.

The Operators are categorized into two (2) parts/ groups that has strategic capabilities that serve a specific purpose in game.

Categories of Rainbow Six (6) Mobile - Operators:

1. Defensive - Defenders
2. Offensive - Attackers

Each Attacking Operator inside Rainbow Six(6) Mobile posses unique specialties used in game that - disarms, free hostages, and breach areas against opponents.

Attackers Unique Abilities:

1. Ash - Breaching Rounds
2. Sledge - Tactical Breaching Hammer
3. Twitch - Shock Drone
4. Thermite - Exothermic Charge
5. Hibana - X-Kairos Launcher

Featured ad spot by google

The Defending Operators focus on a whole different aspect in game that benefits the team by specializing in these fields - Reinforce walls, trap enemies, and protect objectives in game.

Defenders Unique Abilities:

1. Caveira - Silent Step; Interrogation
2. Bandit - Shock Wire
3. Smoke - Remote Gas Grenade
4. Valkyrie - Black Eye Cameras
5. Mute - GC90 Signal Disruptor "Jammer"

So with each operator possessing skillsets that are unique in game for tactical purposes - Rainbow Six (6) Mobile is expected to replicate it's successes on the mobile platform, enhancing the experience.
