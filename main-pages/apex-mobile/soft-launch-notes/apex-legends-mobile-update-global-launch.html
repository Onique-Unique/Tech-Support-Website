In game announcement made mention of Apex Legends Mobile plans to close it's progression and have a reset of purchasing power at the end of the limited regional soft launch.

EA has also stated that Prices on items in game may change during the coming worldwide launch, however at the time of purchasing closure - Purchased Currency will be unavailable.

Important: To gain access to previously owned in game assets and purchases - players must use the same account used during the limited regional launch when logged in at global launch to retreive and attain previously acquired assets.
