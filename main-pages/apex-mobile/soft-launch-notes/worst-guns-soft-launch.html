Top 5 Worst Guns to use in Apex Legends Mobile Soft Launch

Community Post

 March 29, 2022

These are the Weapons you need to AVOID right now in "Apex Legends Mobile" Soft Launch.

What would a Best Weapons List be without a Worse Weapon List to match, They pretty much go hand in hand - quite literally.

Featured ad spot by google

Frosty Mate" came through yet again guiding you on how to choose your weapons, however in this case - what to avoid

Here are The "Top 5 Worst Guns in Apex Legends Mobile" suggested by Frosty Mate.


Apex Legends Mobile Worst Weapons in Soft Launch

5. "P2020" Pistol - No one should be caught near this gun as the accuracy on this thing can be a real certified phone smasher.

Unlike other weapons - using this Gun isn't the best thing to go for especially in a late fight as majority of the time you'll be shooting at everything else other than the target.

Please Note: This list Case was performed under high ping environment within an out of range region; These stats are subjected to change.

4. "Charge Rifle" - A pretty decent weapon to have only if there's no alternatives, This thing will really beam (No pun intended) only if you manage to lock onto a target.

However in fast paced environments - this will dissapoint easily as targets move around frequently; leaving you with no time to charge for a clear shot. Most cases you are likely to miss while using this weapon.

3. "RE-45" - Not bad this one actually - "ok i spoke too soon"; This Gun more than likely ranks the lowest amongst the Light Weapon category, just for this reason.

It has the "Lowest" Mag Capacity, meaning - you are basically roaming around with a display item only for show - "NO" Substance. In addition to that - this thing Boasts the Lowest range, at this point it would be easier to kiss your opponents || Pause.

2. "Mozambique" - With this weapon i can't help but hear the popular catchphrase "Mozambique here!" - other from that, this Gun offers more liability than actually being a valuable asset to have in your possession - here's exactly why:

You have to rely heavily on aiming down sight in close range combat as the hipfire is basically redundant. Well based on that, you're probably saying - well that's not actually too bad. Well - like an infomercial; There's More.

The slow fire rate alone makes you a deer in headlights as you are majority exposed - just for the fact you require close combat to succeed with this weapon coupled with it's slow fire rate with your recoil spewing in all directions.

1. "Hemlock" - This one isn't necessarily bad bad - emphasis on "bad". It just requires practice and positional awareness, This Gun has the occasional pauses that "Burst" Rifles are known for.

As long as you are able to hit your shots then you're fine, otherwise you'll need to just take what's coming for you. The Burst Mode can be pretty annoying if you are not aware of how best to utilize this weapon.

For a long time this Gun has been calling for a buff it hadn't received yet, So it would be wise to go for a fully automatic weapon here.
