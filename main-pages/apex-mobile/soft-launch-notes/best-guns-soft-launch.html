Apex Legends Mobile Top Weapons in Soft Launch

5. "Longbow DMR" Sniper - A super powerful weapon that deals damage enough to make your opponents rethink ever engaging in a combat with you.
The fact this weapon has hardly any recoil makes a real contender for noobs such as myself to use comfortably.

Please Note: In this list i will not be going in depth as to what each weapon provides and it's complete stats, This list is general guidance as to the Top Performing weapons currently. After going through this list you can get indepth knowledge of each weapon by clicking the thumbnail item above to check out "Frosty Mates" video.

4. "L Star EMG" - The beauty about this weapon is that there is no reload required - YES ! you heard that right No Reload. Coupled with a good extended mag - you are on your way to wreak havoc on the lobby.
The downside to this weapon is that it has a cool down function before you can start firing and engaging in combat again.

3. "R99 SMG" - It is with uncertainty, without a doubt this monster makes it on the list as this kid is not to play with. This truly posess the capabilities to beam the clothes off your back, even in a friendly match.
With it's short to medium range ability with firing rate that shreds through enemies like butter, this thing is definately one to add to your arsenal.

2. "Flatline" - What would this list be without the Flatline making an appearance. Ever since the Soft Launch began - this has been a very popular go to weapon for many especially Pro Player's, This thing deals serious damage.

"Frosty Mate" mentioned this weapon was close to taking that number one spot, an overall well balanced weapon that boasts a range that is much better than many other weapons in the gallery.
