How to Draw Anime Girls with Short Hair for Beginners

Anime girls with short hair can be just as pretty as those with long hair. In this tutorial, we'll show you how to draw a simple anime girl with short hair.

  • Start by drawing a basic outline of the head and neck.
  • Next, draw in the facial features. For the eyes, start by drawing a large oval shape. Inside the oval, draw a smaller oval for the pupil.
  • Next, draw the eyebrows and eyelashes. For the eyebrows, draw a thin line above the eyes. For the eyelashes, draw a few short lines above the eyes.
  • Draw the hair. For this tutorial, we'll be drawing short hair, so don't worry about drawing lots of hair. Just draw a few short lines to represent the hair.
  • Finally, color in the anime girl's hair and clothes. And that's it! You've now drawn an anime girl with short hair.



How to Draw Anime Girls with Short Hair realistically

There are a few things you need to remember when drawing anime girls with short hair realistically.

  • For starters, short hair is not just one length; it can be short in the back and longer in the front, or shorter on the sides and longer in the middle, etc.
  • So, when drawing short hair, you need to be aware of the different lengths and how they'll affect the overall look of the hairstyle.
  • Another thing to consider is the texture of the hair. Short hair can be spiky, wavy, or straight, and each type of hair has its own unique look.
  • So, if you want your anime girl's hair to look realistic, you need to be aware of the different hair textures and how to draw them correctly.
  • Lastly, don't forget to add some personality to your character's hairstyle. Just because they have short hair doesn't mean they can't have an interesting or unique hairstyle. So, be creative and have fun with it!
